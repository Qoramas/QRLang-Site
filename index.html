Welcome to QRLang - Samuel Haycock!

QRLang was created to allow developers a quick and easy means to create and distribute games to anyone with a smartphone. QRLang is a game development language that is highly compressible, so much so that it is designed to fit onto a QR code. For those who do not know, a QR code is similar to a bar code that fits on the side of almost any bought product but it is 2 dimensional allowing much more data to be fit onto it. These QR codes can then be scanned with the camera of any android smart device and the compressed game that is encoded onto the QR code will play instantly without the need for downloading or any data connection at all.
